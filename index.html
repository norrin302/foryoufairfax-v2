What We Stand For

Shared priorities that unite all three candidates

🏛️

Transparent Government

Open council meetings, accessible public records, and regular town halls — keeping every resident informed and involved in the decisions that shape our city.

🌳

Sustainable Future

Protecting our parks, green spaces, and waterways while investing in environmental initiatives that reduce our footprint and preserve Fairfax's natural charm.

🤝

Community First

Building bridges across neighborhoods and generations — united in the belief that Fairfax works best when its leaders work together, not against each other.
